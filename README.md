# 📚 B.O.B — Book of Books

> *Distilling the Book of Books: Where psychology and biology meet tech entrepreneurship.*

A curated, no-fluff distillation of the most impactful books across psychology, entrepreneurship, biology, communication, and general knowledge — written by a builder, for builders.

If you've ever wanted the core insight of a book without spending 6 hours reading filler, this is for you.

## Why Read This?

Most books are 10 pages of insight stretched into 300 pages. This document rips out the signal and throws away the noise.

But it's more than a summary collection. The real value is in **how the ideas connect**:

- The habit loop in *Atomic Habits* mirrors the product design loop in *Hooked*
- The neuroplasticity in *You Are Not Your Brain* gives a biological foundation to the mindset shifts in *Mindset* by Carol Dweck
- The brutally honest culture of Israeli startups (*Startup Nation*) echoes the radical honesty principle in *The Get Things Done Book*
- Naval Ravikant's long-term thinking pairs naturally with the monopoly-building framework in *Zero to One*

Read it linearly or jump around. Either way you'll walk away with mental models that compound.

## What's Inside

### 🧠 Psychology & Self-Help
Covers habit formation, manipulation tactics, personality frameworks, courage, and the science of changing your mind. Includes distillations of:
- *Atomic Habits* — James Clear
- *Think Again* — Adam Grant
- *Mindset* — Dr. Carol S. Dweck
- *In Sheep's Clothing* — George Simon
- *Games People Play* — Eric Berne
- and more...

**Highlight:** Adam Grant's insight that high-IQ people are *more* biased — not less — because they're better at finding patterns that confirm what they already believe. Question everything, including yourself.

---

### 🚀 Entrepreneurship
The real mechanics of building companies — from zero to one, from idea to scale, from pitch to funding. Includes distillations of:
- *Zero to One* — Peter Thiel
- *The Hard Thing About Hard Things* — Ben Horowitz
- *Build* — Tony Fadell
- *Hooked* — Nir Eyal
- *Rework* — Jason Fried & DHH
- and more...

**Highlight:** Thiel's four pillars for going big — proprietary technology that's 10x better, network effects, economies of scale, and brand identity. Most startups fail because they only have one or two.

---

### 🧬 Brain & Biology
The science of how your brain actually works — dopamine, neuroplasticity, aging, genetics. Includes distillations of:
- *DOSE* — TJ Power
- *Genome* — Matt Ridley
- *The Brain Book* — Phil Dobson
- *You Are Not Your Brain* — Jeffrey Schwartz & Rebecca Gladding
- *The Death of Death* — José Luis Cordeiro & David Wood

**Highlight:** 90% of serotonin is produced in the gut. What you eat isn't just a physical health decision — it's a mental health decision.

---

### 🗣️ Communication
How to talk to anyone, persuade effectively, and speak publicly with confidence. Includes distillations of:
- *How to Talk to Anyone* — Leil Lowndes
- *The Power of Persuasion* — Rudy Nash
- Public speaking frameworks

**Highlight:** When you make a mistake, don't just apologize — over-repay to the point they're glad it happened. Accountability that goes beyond expectation builds more trust than never failing.

---

### 🌍 General Knowledge
From Russian culture to Israeli startup DNA to the hidden patterns in everyday behaviour.
- *Small Data* — Martin Lindstrom
- *Startup Nation* — Dan Senor & Saul Singer

**Highlight:** Israeli military culture — flat hierarchy, brutal honesty, failure as a badge of honour — is directly responsible for Israel punching far above its weight in global tech. Culture isn't a soft thing. It's infrastructure.
